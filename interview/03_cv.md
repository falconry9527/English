## iZi Finance
```
iZi Finance is a multi-chain DeFi protocol providing One-Stop Liquidity service. As part of the system, iZiSwap is a DEX on the ethereum blockchain, designed to provide efficient, low-slippage trading and liquidity management for digital assets.

Contracts
• Implemented on-chain smart contracts for DEX : mint (add liquidity), burn (remove liquidity), collect (withdraw tokens) and swap (token trading).
Backend
• Fetched on-chain events (pool creation, mint, burn, swap, collect), persisted them in databases, and provided API services (Liquidity,Volume,Pool list,Trade list)
• Built iZiSwap-SDK-go to estimate prices  and slippage by simulating token swaps

```
